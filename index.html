Commuting around Australia

Let's take a look at commutes around the country.

Hobart is the most expensive capital city for commuting at $22.38.

Sydney has the longest average commute time at 62 minutes.

While Brisbane has the longest commute distance of any capital city at 32km.

We're now back in Hobart for the shortest distance commute in a captial city at 26km.

While Darwin has both the cheapest average commute at $17 and the shortest commute time at 36 minutes out of all the capital cities.
